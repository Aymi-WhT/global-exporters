About Us

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

Global Exporters was founded by Abdul Rahman Khan in 2003 with a singular vision: to make international trade accessible, reliable, and efficient for businesses of all sizes. Serving global trade since 2003, we have grown into a trusted name in garment and bridal wear exports from India — primarily serving the Middle East, including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and beyond.

With over 20 years of experience navigating the complexities of global commerce, our team delivers end-to-end trade solutions backed by deep regulatory knowledge and an extensive network of partners worldwide.

Import Services

We source and import quality goods from global markets, ensuring compliance with destination country regulations.

  • Import documentation & compliance
  • Quality inspection & control
  • Duty optimisation & tariff planning
  • Port-to-door delivery coordination

Export Services

We help Indian and regional businesses expand globally by managing the full export lifecycle with expertise.

  • Export licensing & DGFT documentation
  • Market entry strategy & pricing
  • Packaging & labelling compliance
  • Letter of credit & payment facilitation
  • Overseas buyer network access

Logistics & Supply Chain

End-to-end logistics management across air, sea, and land — tailored to your timeline and budget.

  • Multimodal freight management
  • Real-time cargo tracking
  • Warehousing & distribution
  • Cold chain & hazmat handling
  • Last-mile delivery solutions

Customs Clearance

Expert customs clearance assistance to ensure your shipments move swiftly and without complication.

  • Liaison with customs authorities
